I help technology companies, IT departments, engineering leaders, and individual professionals improve communication, execution, leadership, and team health through practical coaching, mentoring, and training. My approach combines real engineering experience, organizational transformation, and hands-on support so improvements actually stick.

You can work with me in two ways: as a partner for your company, or as a coach for your personal growth as an engineer, lead, or manager. In both cases, the goal is the same: reduce friction, strengthen leadership, and build better ways of working.


# For companies.

I work with technology companies and internal IT departments that want healthier teams, clearer leadership, and more reliable execution. This is especially useful when delivery is slowing down, communication is breaking, managers are struggling, or team culture is suffering during growth or change.

Then present the services like this:

## Team and leadership diagnosis

A focused assessment to identify the main sources of friction in your teams, leadership structure, communication, and execution model. This is ideal when you know something is wrong, but you need clarity before acting.

What you get:

- A clear view of the biggest pain points.
- Root-cause analysis across people, processes, and team dynamics.
- A practical improvement roadmap with priorities.
- Recommendations for training, mentoring, and organizational changes.

## Engineering leadership coaching

Coaching and mentoring for engineering managers, team leads, heads of engineering, and emerging leaders. This helps leaders improve communication, delegation, accountability, decision-making, and team development.

What you get:

- 1:1 mentoring sessions.
- Support with difficult team situations.
- Guidance on feedback, alignment, and leadership habits.
- Help building healthier and higher-performing teams.

## Team workshops

Interactive workshops for engineering teams and leaders focused on the soft skills that most affect delivery and collaboration. These sessions are practical, contextualized for technical teams, and designed to create immediate changes in behavior.

Topics can include:

- Communication and alignment.
- Ownership and accountability.
- Productive meetings and decision-making.
- Conflict management.
- Cross-team collaboration.
- Team health and sustainable performance.

## Ways of working and process design

Hands-on support to define or improve team processes so they are clear, usable, and adapted to your real context. This fits well when teams are scaling, reorganizing, or struggling with delivery predictability.

What you get:

- Clear process definitions.
- Practical operating agreements for teams and leaders.
- Improved meeting structures and communication flows.
- Support rolling out the new way of working.

## Training programs for engineering organizations

Tailored training programs for IT departments and tech organizations that want to grow leadership and collaboration capabilities in a structured way. This is useful when one-off workshops are not enough and the company needs a broader learning path.

Formats:

- Single workshops.
- Multi-session programs.
- Leadership cohorts.
- Team-specific enablement tracks.
- Follow-up mentoring and shadowing.

# For individuals.

I also work with individual professionals who want to grow as engineers, team leads, managers, or technical leaders. This is for people who are excellent on the technical side and want to become stronger in communication, influence, leadership, and day-to-day effectiveness.

## Career and leadership coaching

For engineers moving into leadership, new managers, or experienced leaders who want an external sounding board. We work on the human side of leadership without losing technical identity.

Typical topics:

- Moving from individual contributor to lead or manager.
- Leading former peers.
- Giving feedback and handling conflict.
- Building confidence and presence.
- Prioritizing better and communicating more clearly.

## Productivity and effectiveness coaching

For professionals who feel overloaded, scattered, or stuck in reactive work. This service helps improve focus, personal organization, communication habits, and sustainable execution. Your site’s Booster Skills framework already gives you a natural angle for this kind of offer.

Typical outcomes:

- Better prioritization.
- Less friction in daily work.
- More clarity in commitments and follow-through.
- Stronger personal systems and habits.

## Mentoring for difficult work situations
Short-term support for people dealing with specific challenges such as unclear expectations, team conflict, low trust, poor manager relationships, or blocked growth. This is a practical mentoring format focused on real situations, not generic advice.

How I work:

1. Discovery — We start with a conversation to understand your context, goals, and current pain points.
2. Diagnosis — I identify the main friction points affecting leadership, communication, execution, or team health.
3. Intervention — We define the right mix of coaching, workshops, mentoring, process design, or training.
4. Follow-through — I stay involved to help the change take root through mentoring, shadowing, and iteration.
